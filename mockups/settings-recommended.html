📘 First time? Connect Interactive Brokers — step by step ▾
A one-time setup that lets the app read (never change) your IBKR statements. About 5 minutes — follow each step exactly.
Step 1 · Log in on a computerOpen the IBKR Client Portal

Go to interactivebrokers.com → Client Portal (web, not the app). Top menu: Performance & Reports → Flex Queries.

Step 2 · Create the queryNew Activity Flex Query

Click the blue + next to "Activity Flex Query". In Query Name type VIV.

Step 3 · Tick two sectionsOpen Positions & Trades

Under Open Positions click Select All. Under Trades click Select All. Leave everything else unticked.

Step 4 · Delivery settingsXML, last 365 days

Format = XML, Period = Last 365 Calendar Days. (We only keep trades from May 2026 on, so nothing recent is missed.)

Step 5 · General settingsLeave the defaults

Date yyyyMMdd, Time HHmmss, Separator ;, all Yes/No = No. Click Continue → Create.

Step 6 · Copy the Query IDWrite down the number

The query now shows a Query ID on the list. Copy it.

Step 7 · Get a tokenFlex Web Service

Find Flex Web Service Configuration, switch Status to on, click Generate New Token (longest expiry), and copy it. Treat it like a password.

Step 8 · Paste & saveLink your account

Paste the Query ID and token into the fields above and click Save connection. Done — privately linked to you. The Sync button now pulls your data.

Good to know: trades appear about 1 business day after they happen (log manually for same-day, then reconcile). Every sync shows a preview first and only adds/updates IBKR rows — it never edits or deletes your manual entries.
